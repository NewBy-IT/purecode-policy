Privacy Policy

PureCode — Code Screenshot & Export

We built PureCode with a simple belief: your code is yours. No tracking, no data collection, no exceptions.

🛡️

Our Commitment

Privacy by design

PureCode is built with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. Everything you do in PureCode stays entirely on your device.

💡

PureCode has no servers, no accounts, no analytics, and no third-party SDKs. Your code and images never leave your device.

✅

What We Do NOT Collect

Absolutely nothing

  • ✓Personal information
  • ✓Code or snippets
  • ✓Usage analytics
  • ✓Crash reports
  • ✓Device identifiers
  • ✓Location data
  • ✓Advertising IDs
  • ✓Email or contacts
🖼️

Photo Library Access

Save only, never read

PureCode requests permission to save images to your Photo Library when you tap "Download". This is the only system permission the app uses.

  • Access is requested only when you explicitly choose to save an image.
  • PureCode uses add-only access — it can save images but cannot read or browse your existing photos.
  • No images are uploaded anywhere. Files are saved locally to your device only.
💾

Local Storage

Stays on your device

PureCode saves your preferences (theme, background, font size, language, etc.) locally on your device using Apple's standard UserDefaults storage.

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

📡

No Network Requests

100% offline

PureCode works entirely offline. The app makes zero network requests. It does not connect to any servers, APIs, or external services.

⚡

Syntax highlighting, image rendering, and all processing happen locally on your device — instantly.

🔗

Third-Party Services

None, zero, nada

PureCode does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics frameworks, advertising networks, or crash reporting tools. There are no third parties involved in the operation of this app.

👶

Children's Privacy

Safe for everyone

PureCode does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Because we collect no data at all, the app is safe for users of any age.

📋

Changes to This Policy

We'll keep you informed

If we ever update this Privacy Policy, we will revise the effective date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

Given that PureCode collects no data, changes to this policy are unlikely to affect your privacy in any way.
